A strong personal brand is built on authenticity, consistency, and value delivery. It requires a clear niche, compelling storytelling, and a visible online presence across the right platforms.

A basic personal brand takes 2–4 weeks. Corporate identity projects for schools and hospitality brands typically run 8–16 weeks depending on scope.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We have active clients across West Africa, the UK, and North America. All engagements can be conducted fully remote.

What deliverables are included?

Standard packages include brand guidelines, logo suite, typography system, color palette, social media templates, and a 60-day support period.
